This is a guide to test the Dapp

  • First create an account by connecting your Metamask wallet in the creators section.
  • After creating the account, you'll be redirected to the creators panel, now you can mint a new musical Nft. You will be required to have some test eth in the Goerli network.
  • Now that you already have created your Nft navigate to the explore panel and click in your artist card to access your own playlist.
  • Congratulations you have minted your first musical Nft on the GoatBeat platform
